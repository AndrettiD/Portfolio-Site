About

Andretti Duvenhage
I'm a Software Engineer in the San Fransisco Bay Area.

My name is Andretti and I’m a sound designer turned software engineer
with a passion for integrating my audio knowledge with unique technology
and code. I have a profound interest in technology and how it has changed
in the last few years. I want to be part of that change and create tools
and applications that can make a difference in the world.

Through my love for learning, I have pushed myself to acquire a variety
of skills such as Coding (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Jquery, React, Ruby, MongoDB, Mongoose, SQL, )
and designing sound for live theater. I chose to become a Software Engineer because
I am always interested
in how things work from the inside out. Through six months of self-study
followed by the Software Engineering Immersive with General Assembly,
I learned how to code in a variety of languages while connecting with
new colleagues from all over the country. I enjoy how software engineering
requires me to work closely with a team to complete satisfying projects.
